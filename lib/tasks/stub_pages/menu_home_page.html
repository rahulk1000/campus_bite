Dining Facilities
Residential Dining Services does not guarantee the accuracy of nutrition information. While we make every effort to ensure our nutrition information is accurate, all information is approximate. Nutrition values and allergens are susceptible to change as deviation from the recipe such as product substitution; food availability, changes to portion sizes, manufacturing and other factors will provide inaccurate information. Individuals with allergies should not rely on NetNutritition as the only source of allergen information. Information provided should not take the place of any medical recommendations and individuals with special dietary needs should not solely use this website to ensure the food provided will meet their specific needs.
